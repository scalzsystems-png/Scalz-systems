FAQs

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Is Scalz Systems for all types of businesses?
Scalz Systems is built specifically for home service businesses — plumbers, HVAC techs, roofers, electricians, landscapers, cleaners, and more. That said, our system works for virtually any local service business that relies on Google, word-of-mouth, and reviews to grow. If you serve customers in a local area, we can help you dominate your market.
Will this really help me get more leads and reviews?
Absolutely. Our clients typically see a 2–4× increase in Google reviews within the first 60 days, and lead volume increases 3× on average within 6 months. The automated review requests alone have helped clients go from under 20 reviews to hundreds. Your results depend on your service area and how actively you use the system, but our 30-day money-back guarantee means there's zero risk to try.
How do the review and text-back automations actually work?
After a job is completed, our system automatically sends a personalized SMS and email to your customer asking them to leave a Google review. It's fully customizable with your branding and tone. For missed calls, any time someone calls your business number and you don't answer, our system fires off an automatic text within seconds — keeping that lead warm before they call a competitor.
How does Scalz Systems handle customer data and privacy?
We take privacy seriously. All customer data is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties. Our systems comply with CAN-SPAM, TCPA, and GDPR guidelines. All SMS campaigns include opt-out options, and we provide clear privacy policies for your customers. You own your data at all times — if you ever leave, you can export everything.
What if I'm not tech-savvy?
You don't need to be. We build and set up everything for you. Our onboarding team handles your website, automations, and integrations from start to finish. After your 25-minute launch call, the system largely runs itself. And whenever you have a question, our support team is just a message away. Most of our clients describe themselves as "not very tech-savvy" — and they're our biggest success stories.
